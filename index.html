AdaptiveGame - Accessible Puzzle Game

Push boxes onto target positions to win. Use arrow keys, voice commands, or screen reader navigation.

Controls: Arrow keys to move. Press R to restart level. Press H for help.

This game is designed to be fully accessible for blind, deaf, and motor-impaired players.
